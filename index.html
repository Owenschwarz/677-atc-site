Not Just a Gym

One Facility, Every Part of the Game

677 ATC is a full athlete development center where players train hockey skills, build their bodies, and track progress through a structured, technology-supported environment.

Skills Lab

Hockey rink area for skating, puck work, and small-area skill development.

Shooting Lab

Rapid Shot-style technology for shot speed, accuracy, and release tracking.

Hands Studio

Stickhandling and puck-control training with Rapid Hands and synthetic ice.

Skating Lab

Synthetic ice for skating mechanics, edge work, and movement development.

Strength & Power

Weight room built for strength, explosiveness, and injury prevention.

Speed & Agility

Sprinting, footwork, change of direction, and coordination training.

Testing Suite

VALD-based testing for force, strength, power, balance, and mobility.

Reception & Pro-Shop

Check-in, retail, gear, and the front-of-house experience.

How Training Works

Assess. Train. Compete.
