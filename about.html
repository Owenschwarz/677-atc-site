About 677 ATC

Not a Gym. Not Just a Skills Rink.

677 ATC is a full athlete development center combining hockey training, strength and conditioning, technology, and performance testing under one roof.

Mission

Develop Stronger, Faster, More Complete Athletes

677 ATC is a state-of-the-art hockey and athletic performance facility built to develop stronger, faster, more skilled, and more complete athletes through a structured, technology-supported environment.

Who It's For

Youth Through Junior & College Prep

677 ATC is built for hockey players at every stage, from youth development through high school, junior, and college prep, as well as adult and recreational athletes looking to train with real structure.

What Makes It Different

Training That's Measured, Not Guessed At

Athletes aren't just training randomly, they're being measured, tracked, and developed. Every program is built around real testing data and technology like VALD, Rapid Shot, and Rapid Hands.

Facility Philosophy

One Facility, Every Part of the Game

Instead of splitting training across a gym, a skills rink, and a shooting facility, 677 ATC combines on-ice skill development, off-ice strength training, shooting technology, stickhandling work, speed training, and performance testing into one facility.
